# Privacy Policy for Happie Couple

**Last Updated: February 10, 2026**

## Introduction

Happie Couple respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Personal Information
- Account Information: Email address, name, profile photo
- Partner Information: Partner's email and name (when linked)
- Profile Data: Relationship status, preferences, notification settings

### Relationship Data
- Daily Check-ins: Mood ratings, notes, timestamps
- Weekly Meetings: Meeting notes, agendas, action items  
- Diary Entries: Private and shared notes, tags, categories
- Date Ideas: Saved date ideas, completed dates
- Calendar Events: Event titles, dates, descriptions

### Usage Information
- App Analytics: Feature usage, screen views, session duration
- Device Information: Device type, operating system, app version
- Crash Reports: Error logs via Firebase Crashlytics

### Information We Do NOT Collect
- Location data
- Browsing history outside the app
- We do NOT sell your personal information
- We do NOT use your data for advertising

## How We Use Your Information

1. **Service Delivery**: Provide and maintain the application
2. **Partner Features**: Enable sharing with your linked partner
3. **AI Insights**: Generate personalized relationship insights
4. **Notifications**: Send reminders based on your preferences
5. **Customer Support**: Respond to questions and provide assistance
6. **Improvement**: Analyze usage patterns to improve features
7. **Security**: Detect and prevent fraud and abuse

## Data Storage and Security

- Data stored securely using Firebase Cloud Firestore (Google Cloud)
- All data encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS) and at rest (AES-256)
- Secure authentication via Firebase Authentication
- Regular security audits and updates
- Data retained as long as account is active
- Deleted accounts: Data permanently deleted within 30 days

## Data Sharing

### With Your Partner
- Data marked as "shared" is visible to your linked partner
- Private data remains visible only to you

### With Third-Party Services
1. Firebase (Google) - Authentication, database, analytics
2. RevenueCat - Subscription processing
3. Google Cloud Functions - Backend processing

We do NOT sell your data to third parties.

## Your Privacy Rights

You have the right to:
- **Access**: View all your personal data
- **Correct**: Update inaccurate information
- **Delete**: Request complete account deletion
- **Export**: Download your data in machine-readable format

Exercise your rights in **Settings > Privacy** or email privacy@happiecouple.app

## Children's Privacy

Happie Couple is NOT intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 18.

## International Users

Data is transferred to, stored, and processed in the United States.

**EU Users (GDPR)**: You have additional rights including data portability and the right to lodge complaints with supervisory authorities.

**California Users (CCPA)**: You have the right to know what data is collected and request deletion. We do not sell personal information.

## Changes to This Policy

We will notify you of material changes by email or in-app notification.

## Contact Us

**Email**: privacy@happiecouple.app
**In-App**: Settings > Help & Support > Contact Us
